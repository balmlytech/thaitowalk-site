Cookie Policy

Last updated 12 June 2026

Cookies are small files a website can store in your browser. This page explains the very limited use of cookies on this site and how you stay in control.

A note for the owner: this reflects the site as built today — no analytics, no advertising, just an optional Google map. If you add online ordering, analytics, a chat widget or social embeds later, this page (and the consent banner) must be updated to list those cookies.

How we use cookies

We don’t use cookies for analytics, advertising or tracking. The only cookies on this site come from the embedded Google map in the “Find us” section — and that map stays blocked until you choose to load it. If you never load the map, no third-party cookies are set.

We also store one small item in your browser to remember your cookie choice so we don’t ask again on every visit. This is strictly necessary and isn’t shared with anyone.

Your choices

  • The banner — on your first visit you can Accept or Reject the optional map cookies. Reject is the default if you do nothing: the map stays blocked.
  • Change your mind anytime — use the link in the footer of any page to reopen the banner.
  • “Load the map” button — if you’ve rejected cookies but want to see the map just once, you can load it directly from the “Find us” section.
  • Your browser — you can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time.
