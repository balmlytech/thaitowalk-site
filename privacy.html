Privacy Policy

Last updated 12 June 2026

Thai To Walk is a Thai street-food takeaway at 94 High Street, Bedford. This policy explains what happens to your information when you use this website.

A note for the owner: this is a tailored template, not legal advice. Before relying on it, confirm the contact details below, your business/trading details, and — if you later add online ordering, a contact form, analytics or a mailing list — have this policy updated to match.

The short version

This website does not collect personal information about you. There are no contact forms, accounts, logins or online ordering. We don’t run analytics or advertising trackers. The only cookies are set by the embedded Google map on our “Find us” section, and only if you choose to load it.

Who we are

Thai To Walk (“we”, “us”) is the data controller for this website. You can reach us at:

  • Thai To Walk, 94 High Street, Bedford, MK40 1NN
  • Email: hello@thaitowalk.com

Information collected automatically

Hosting

Our website is hosted by Netlify. Like all web hosts, Netlify’s servers automatically process basic technical information needed to deliver the site and keep it secure — for example your IP address, browser type and the pages requested. This is standard server activity and is not used to identify you.

Fonts

We load typefaces from Google Fonts. To serve them, Google may receive your IP address. Google Fonts does not set cookies through this site.

The map

Our “Find us” section can show a Google map. The map is blocked until you choose to load it. If you load it, Google sets cookies in your browser. See our Cookie Policy for details and how to control this.

Legal bases

Under UK GDPR we rely on: your consent for loading the Google map and its cookies; and our legitimate interests in running a secure, working website (the technical data above).

Sharing your information

We do not sell your data. The only third parties involved are the service providers that make the site work:

  • Netlify — website hosting.
  • Google — Google Fonts and (only if you load it) Google Maps.

These providers process data on servers that may be outside the UK; they operate under their own privacy terms and appropriate safeguards.

Retention

We don’t hold a database of website visitors. Technical server logs are kept by our host only for as long as needed for security and operation. Your cookie choice is stored in your own browser until you clear it.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, erase or restrict the use of personal data we hold about you, and to object to processing. As we don’t collect personal data through this site, in most cases we’ll have nothing on file — but you’re welcome to contact us with any request.

If you have a concern you can also contact the UK regulator, the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO), at ico.org.uk.

Children

This site is a general menu and information page and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes

If we change how the site works (for example by adding online ordering), we’ll update this policy and the “last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@thaitowalk.com or write to us at 94 High Street, Bedford, MK40 1NN.
